solyto is a free, private, all-in-one personal management app — covering your todos, contacts, calendar, notes, news, music and book library in one place, with one login, and one coherent interface.

Connect to your own self-hosted solyto instance or use solyto.app.

<b>Features</b>

<ul>
<li>Todos with one-line creation and short commands</li>
<li>Calendar with DAV sync</li>
<li>Contacts with DAV sync</li>
<li>Notebook — folders, markdown, WYSIWYG editor</li>
<li>News reader</li>
<li>Music, Book, Game, Recipe, Quote and Link libraries</li>
<li>Check In — track your mood and health</li>
<li>Time Tracking with Pomodoro Timer</li>
<li>Finance Tracking — income and wealth</li>
<li>Clipboard — copy things across devices</li>
<li>Dashboard combining all your data</li>
<li>Multiple themes including Skyrim, paper, and Atari</li>
</ul>

<b>Highlights</b>

<ul>
<li>Import music from Deezer or Discogs, books from Hardcover or Goodreads</li>
<li>Get notified about new releases from favorite artists or authors</li>
<li>Automatically save links via Telegram Bot</li>
<li>Daily digest notifications via Telegram, Email or Push</li>
</ul>

<b>Privacy</b>

No tracking, no analytics, no cookies, no AI, no subscriptions. solyto only contacts external services when you explicitly request it.
